Bobby’s response to DICKROD and BARB

My preference would be no changes in 2020.

But i don't really have any issue with adding an extra roster spot to keep up with MLB, so I'd likely vote yes.

For BARB + DICKROD i do think we'd be wise to alter pitcher scoring, but it's such a deep issue and so dependant on how the MLB continues to evolve, that i wonder if we dont know the best way forward on P categories. Holds are hella weird, especially in the first year of 3 batter minimums. Quality Starts are great, but we're in the era of the opener. So I worry that we'd be replacing a problem with another problem, and might be best served to hold our dick(rod)s until an obvious better option emerges.

All that said I love the pitching side of the ball and would be stoked for less Luck to influence our P stats, so i could be persuaded.

Spencer on DICKROD and BARB

I'm leaning against DICKROD because QS is milquetoast. Plus, I've always appreciated how W & L cut against each other. They penalize equally those who sit on their hands and those who pick up streamers for every day of the week.

There's a stronger case to replacing SV with SV+HLD. For one, MLB teams are moving away from traditional closer roles, why shouldn't we? With fewer shitty relievers in lineups, pitching would be more competitive across the board. Our lineups would be better too if we collectively spent less draft capital on explosive assets that won't be on my roster for more than a few months. Finally, elite closers are still the best assets in SV+HLD. Closers tend to have more reliable workloads and should continue to give owners big stats.
